About Market Monster

A stock market trading site where money is fake but the competition is real.



Who we are

Have you ever wanted to know more about investment in the stock market? Well, so did we. That's why we created Market Monster.To learn in a risk-free environment about how to invest while having some fun in the process.





It's Your Money

Individuals taking responsibility for their own stock investments is nothing new. There are countless sites and apps dedicated to helping the 'average Joe' invest their hard earned money. We operate under the assumption that you may not be ready to put your money on the line just yet. Or maybe you have already started investing but want to try some untested stocks to see how well they might do for a short time. This is the perfect place to get started.





"The only source of knowledge is experience"
-Albert Einstein

With Market Monster you will have the resources to thoroughly investigate a stock before risk becomes an issue. A deep understanding of the marketplace and experience are the most sought after qualities in an investor. Your wouldn't trust a complete novice with investing your life savings. Nonetheless that is exactly what countless people are doing every day when they decide to invest on their own. Don't lose money just 'learning the ropes'. Gain the vital experience you need here, at Market Monster.





But investment is supposed to be boring, right?

Wrong, many people thrive on the excitment of watching their investments prosper while also being tortured by their poor investments. Here, we tend to focus on the fun. If you've ever played or even heard of fantasy sports like fantasy football and the like, you are familiar with the sort of friendly rivalry we foster here. Market Monster is also a social marketplace where you can get advice, compare growth and revel in the genius of your latest acquisition. Earn the respect of your fellow investors or learn from their achievements and mistakes. Come share your failures and triumphs with a community focused on both finance and fun.
